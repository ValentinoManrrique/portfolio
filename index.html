ABOUT ME

Valentino Manrrique

I'm Valentino — a designer, developer, and sound nerd from Argentina. I studied Computer Science at Universidad Nacional de Córdoba, earned a UX certification from Acámica, and spent time at Readz in LA building digital publishing tools that taught me how design and engineering work best when they're inseparable.

Outside of client work, I'm deep into audio — producing beats in FL Studio, DJing, and exploring how sound design shapes the way people experience digital products. That obsession with sensory detail is what drives my approach to UX: every interaction should feel right, not just look right.

I'm also fascinated by game development — the intersection of real-time systems, interactive design, and storytelling is where I find the most creative inspiration. And if you're wondering about the long-term plan: Japan. Living and working there is the goal I'm building toward, one project at a time.

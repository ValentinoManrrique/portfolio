01 // WHAT_I_DELIVER

End-to-end design, from blank canvas to dev-ready files

Whether you're launching a new product or redesigning an existing one, I handle the full design lifecycle. That means research, information architecture, component libraries, and production-ready Figma files — not just pretty mockups.

Every design decision is justified. I don't design in a vacuum — I reference your users, your goals, and your constraints at every step.

02 // THE_PROCESS

How a typical UI/UX engagement runs

01

Discovery & Research

User interviews, competitor analysis, and heuristic evaluation. Defines what to solve before we start designing.

02

Information Architecture

Sitemaps, user flows, and content hierarchy. Ensures the structure is right before any UI work begins.

03

Wireframes → Hi-Fi UI

Low-fidelity layouts validated first, then elevated to full visual designs with your brand identity applied.

04

Component Library

A scalable Figma component system — buttons, forms, cards, modals — built for your team to extend.

05

Interactive Prototype & Handoff

Clickable prototype for stakeholder sign-off, then annotated dev-ready specs delivered via Figma.

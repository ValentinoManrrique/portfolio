01 // OVERVIEW

AI generation that replaces (most of) the studio shoot

For e-commerce brands, traditional product photography is expensive and slow to iterate. AI generation changes the math — you can produce dozens of variations in hours, test different backgrounds and contexts, and only commission traditional photography when you actually need it.

Not a replacement for all photography. A strategic tool that dramatically reduces iteration costs and time-to-publish for brands.

02 // THE_PROCESS

From brief to final assets

01

Product Brief + Brand Assets

Understand the product, brand guidelines, and target contexts. Collect any existing product images to use as references.

02

AI Generation (Midjourney / DALL·E 3)

Generate multiple variations — different backgrounds, lighting setups, and lifestyle contexts. Typically 20-40 initial outputs.

03

Selection + Direction Refinement

Client selects preferred directions. Iterate on the best ones with refined prompts and parameters.

04

Photoshop Cleanup + Brand Consistency

Post-process finals in Photoshop — remove artifacts, apply brand color grading, ensure consistency across the set.

05

Final Delivery

Deliver high-resolution files optimized for web and print. Include source files for future iterations.
